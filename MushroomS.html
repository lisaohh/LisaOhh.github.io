Mushroom Stroganoff

serves 2

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2-3 garlic gloved, minced
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 300g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 180ml vegan beef broth
  • 180ml oat milk
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp chilli flakes
  • 2 tbsp nooch
  • Salt & Pepper, to taste
  • tarragon, to taste
