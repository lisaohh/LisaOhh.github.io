Best Sweet n Sour

serves 2

Ingredients

For the sauce

  • 1/2 cup caster sugar
  • 1/3 cup pineapple juice
  • 1/4 cup white wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup tomato ketchup
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic powder

For the dish

  • Chicken
  • 1/3 green pepper
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 1 carrot
  • mushrooms (3)

Instructions

  1. Mix all sauce ingredients in a bowl and whisk
  2. Add chicken pieces to a hot pan and let it simmer
  3. Chop all veg and add into pan
  4. Let simmer for 25-30 mins or until the sauce thickens
  5. Serve with chips or rice
  6. Enjoy!
